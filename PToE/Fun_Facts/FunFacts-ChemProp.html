Chemical Property Fun Facts

  • Helium is the only element discovered with known states of matter that does not have a freezing/condensing point. Yes, Helium cannot become solid.
  • Tungsten has the highest melting point of any discovered element, reaching a whopping 3,422 C°. However it does not have the highest boiling point, Rhenium takes the trophy on that one at 5,596 C°.
  • The heaviest natural element (as in one atom) is Uranium, with 92 Protons.
  • Hydrogen has the smallest number of Isotopes at 3 total, while Xenon and Cesium tie for 1st at a grand total of 36 Isotopes.
