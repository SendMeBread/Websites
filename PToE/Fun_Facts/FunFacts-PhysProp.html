Physical Property Fun Facts:

  • Osmium wins as the densest element, at 22,590 kg/m3
  • Tungsten has the highest melting point of any discovered element, reaching a whopping 3,422 C°. However it does not have the highest boiling point, Rhenium takes the trophy on that one at 5,596 C°.
